Welcome to Ayursakthi

Ayurshakthi Ayurvedic centre is a multi specialty ayurvedic treatment centre located at N.Parvur in Ernakulam dist. of Kerala

We follow the authentic treatment regime with genuine medication.

We are a franchise of the well renowned Dhanwantari Vaidyasala which was established in 1933.

Ayurshakthi ayurvedic centre offers specialized ayurvedic treatment in the fields of ophthalmology, spine and arthritis, gynaecology, paediatrics,

geriatrics, mental ailments etc. We also provide rejuvenation and relaxation treatment which will leave you revitalized.

The treatment centre is located at a distance of 20km from Nedumabaserry airport, 15Km from the Alwaye railway station and 5km from Cherai beach.
